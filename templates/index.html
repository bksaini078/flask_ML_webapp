Mean Teacher

In the mean teacher model, two identical models are trained with two different strategies called student and teacher model. In which, only student model is trained, however, during training exponential moving weights are assigned to the teacher hence it is called as Mean teacher.In our project, we are evaluating this approach in text classification problem using four different noise strategies.

  • With Synonym as noise
  • With Dropouts
  • With Synonym and Dropouts
